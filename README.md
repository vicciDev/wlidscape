# wildScape
*A prototype for discovering, documenting, and sharing the wildlife around you.*

 **Live Prototype:** [WildScape](wildscape-app.netlify.app/)

wildScape is an early-stage concept for a nature-focused social app built to celebrate outdoor discoveries. This prototype gives you a glimpse of how wildlife enthusiasts could log their sightings, explore what others are finding, and connect through a calm, visual, and community-centered experience.

# What you can do in this prototype
## Capture moments in nature

Try out how you might log a wildlife sighting-add a photo, mark a location, and jot down a quick note. It’s a simple preview of how sharing could feel in the full version.

## Explore a discovery feed

Browse an example feed filled with recent and trending wildlife posts. The design focuses on large visuals, clean layouts, and a sense of immersion in nature.

## Get a feel for community

Preview how users could interact through comments, likes, and profiles. These interactions are conceptual, offering a sense of the social layer wildScape aims to build.

## Experience a nature-first design

The interface is made to be calm, modern, and accessible-optimized for both mobile adventurers and desktop explorers. Smooth transitions and responsive layouts help bring the concept to life.

# About WildScape
This is not a fully functional app.
The prototype focuses on:
* User experience
* Visual design and navigation
* Early interaction concepts
* Exploring what a nature social platform could become
Core functionality accounts, real posts, live data, and backend features—is intentionally limited or not yet implemented.

# Why wildScape?

Nature is full of stories. wildScape aims to create a space where people can share those moments-spotting a bird on a morning walk, discovering tracks on a trail, or simply pausing to appreciate something wild.

This prototype is the first step toward imagining a community built around curiosity, connection, and the joy of being outdoors.
